Solveiga Mykolaityte

Solveiga Mykolaityte is a Lithuanian professional actresses/model, known as one of the very successful supermodels belonging from Lithuania. Solveiga Mykolaityte was born and brought up in a small town in Lithuania. She faced many challenges and had to struggle a lot at the beginning of her career. At the age of 15 years, she went to her first modeling Audition but was rejected due to her overly athletic figure.
